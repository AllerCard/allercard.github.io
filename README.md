## About AllerCard

AllerCard shows your food allergy to a chef in any of the 24 official EU languages. Offline. One-time purchase. No accounts, no tracking, no cloud.

Allergen names are sourced from EU Regulation 1169/2011, Annex II — the legal text on every food label in the EU. Sentence-level phrases are formal-register drafts pending native-speaker verification, and the app discloses this honestly.
